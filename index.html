Our Story

More Than Just a Bakery

Nestled in the heart of Khategaon, Pinky Bakery has earned its place as a beloved neighbourhood institution. Walk in any morning and you're greeted by the warm scent of freshly baked bread, the sight of delicate pastries lined in neat rows, and smiles that make you feel right at home.

From hand-decorated celebration cakes to everyday chai-time snacks, every product is prepared with genuine care. The kitchen runs on simple principles: fresh ingredients, honest flavours, and zero compromises on quality.

Whether you're picking up a quick veg puff on your way to work, or choosing a custom cake for a special occasion, Pinky Bakery promises a warm, welcoming experience every single time.
